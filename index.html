About Startup Jaipur

Startup Jaipur is a comprehensive Startup Program designed to empower the youth and entrepreneurs of Rajasthan. We provide a structured pathway for students, early-stage founders, and local businesses to launch and scale their ventures successfully.

Our primary mission is to bridge the gap between traditional business wisdom and modern technology. We serve as a dedicated ecosystem that offers mentorship, funding opportunities, and legal support to turn job seekers into job creators.

We understand that starting a business is daunting, especially without the right guidance. That is why we have curated a 360-degree support system that handles everything from the initial spark of an idea to the final execution and scaling.

Our program offers three core pillars of support: Mentorship, Funding, and Legal Assistance. We connect you with industry veterans who have walked the path before, ensuring you don't make the same mistakes. Our network of Angel Investors and Venture Capitalists is specifically looking for the next big unicorn from Tier-2 cities. Furthermore, we remove the bureaucratic hurdles by providing free legal and CA support for company registration and compliance.

Whether you are a college student with a revolutionary tech idea, a housewife looking to start a D2C brand, or a local shopkeeper wanting to go digital, Startup Jaipur is your partner in growth. We provide co-working spaces, technical workshops, and networking events that foster collaboration. Join us in this journey to put Jaipur on the global startup map. Let's build the future, together.
