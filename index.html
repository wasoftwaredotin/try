Who We Are

Bridging Heritage with
Innovation

Startup Jaipur is not just an incubator; it is a movement. Located in the heart of India's Pink City, we are dedicated to transforming traditional businesses with modern technology while nurturing the next generation of tech unicorns.

We provide a platform where artisans meet app developers, and students meet investors. Our mission is to put Jaipur on the global startup map.

Our Vision

"Empowering Dreams, Building the Future."

Startup Jaipur aims to empower students, college girls and boys, job seekers, and young entrepreneurs to start and grow modern, technology-driven startups.

Our vision is to make Jaipur and Rajasthan a strong startup ecosystem by providing the right ideas, guidance, and execution support.

With the right mentorship, every young person can become a successful entrepreneur.
