About Us

Startup Jaipur is a startup program meticulously crafted to turn the vibrant dreams of our youth into reality. We bridge the critical divide between ancestral business acumen and modern exponential technology.

Our mandate is to transform a generation of job seekers into visionary job creators by offering a high-performance incubation pathway right here in the heart of Rajasthan.

The Jaipur Entrepreneurial Gazette

Volume XXVI | Special Edition Dateline: Jaipur, Rajasthan Thursday, February 12, 2026

Everaging the historical entrepreneurial spirit of the Marwari community with the high-velocity requirements of the 21st-century tech ecosystem, Startup Jaipur is defining the modern standard for Tier-2 economic growth. Based within the architectural marvel of the Manhattan Riviera at the Sitapura Industrial Area, our physical hub is more than just an office—it is a pressure-cooker for innovation where heritage meets hardware.

We understand that starting a business is an inherently daunting prospect. The bureaucratic labyrinth, the isolation of a solo founder, and the persistent "Capital Gap" often kill revolutionary ideas before they even reach a prototype stage. This is why we have engineered a 360-degree support architecture that meticulously handles every phase of the startup lifecycle.

The Three Pillars of Empire Building

Our program operates on three foundational pillars: Mentorship, Funding, and Institutional Compliance. We bridge the gap by connecting founders with industry veterans who have successfully navigated the "Valley of Death."

In terms of capital, our synergy with the Rajasthan Government's iStart Policy is unmatched. We assist our residents in securing non-dilutive government grants, while simultaneously plugging them into an elite network of Angel Investors and VCs.

"Democratizing the startup dream means ensuring a student from Jaipur has the same resources as a graduate from Stanford."

Inclusive Growth: A Social Revolution

What truly distinguishes Startup Jaipur is our commitment to Inclusive Innovation. We believe that the digital economy should belong to everyone. Our dedicated 'Growth Tracks' are tailored for diverse segments: ambitious college students, housewives launching premium D2C brands, and traditional local shopkeepers.

We remove the "Bureaucratic Friction" by providing Free Legal and CA Support. Every resident of the Hub is groomed for resilience. We are not just building startups; we are building the future of the Indian economy from the ground up.

Our mission transcends business—it is about putting Jaipur on the global innovation map.

Let's build the future, together.
